Valley of the Kings

Why Should You Go?

"An exquisite place to see!"
"Remarkable inscriptions on the Tombs make it worth the visit!"
"It's amazing to revisit a part of history!"

The Ancient Tombs of the Valley of the Kings make for an amazing vacation full of history and education! These crypts, located on the hills of Luxor, were first made by New Kingdom Pharoahs in 1500 B.C. There are 63 tombs, the latest being discovered in 2008 by archaeologist, Otto Schaden. It holds the historical burial sit of King Tutankhamun, as well as mummies of ancient kings and queens. Come to see these tombs and witness astonishing hieroglyphics written thousands of years ago! There is a lot to do here!

  • Explore the Tombs
  • Visit the Hatshepsut Temple
  • Walk the hills of Luxor

When you finish checking out the Valley, try out these places near by!

  • Go to the Eye of Horus Tour in Luxor (0.4 miles away)
  • Visit the Luxor Museum (3.5 miles away)
  • Luxor Sound and Light show (3.6 miles away)
